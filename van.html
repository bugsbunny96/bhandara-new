BHANDARA - VAN

'Serving Best Meals On Four Wheels!'

Shree Mata Mansa Devi Bhandara Committee Charitable Trust has served Bhandara for many years on temple premises. After experiencing huge devotion of people towards enjoying holy Bhandara, the committee decided on the idea of distributing food in vans.

The concept of serving food in a four-wheeler started with just two vans. On the inauguration of the last two vans, Shri Gian Chand Gupta Ji (Vidhan Sabha Speaker, Haryana) donated two more vans from his GQ funds. Currently there are eight vans in total, which are being used to distribute food across different locations in Tricity every day. On the inauguration of the last two vans, Shri Gian Chand Gupta Ji (Vidhan Sabha Speaker, Haryana) donated two more vans from his GQ funds.

The Bhandara vans carry delicious and fine quality foods prepared in the temple's kitchen with total hygiene. These vans run across a 60 km radius of Tricity. Each van is capable of carrying food for 500+ people. These vans run across areas of Tricity, like slums, sector 32 GMCH Chandigarh, PGI, and Sector 6 Panchkula Hospital, as per the requirements. Anyone willing to donate can book these with advance bookings of 10-15 days.

The demands of people for Bhandara are sufficiently fulfilled, but the Bhandara committee keeps trying to modify the facilities with the big vision of serving more & more people.
